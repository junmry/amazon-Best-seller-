三、SEO 埋词建议
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

【必埋核心词】（每处都要出现）
- sofa / couch
- sectional（如适用）
- living room

【标题优先埋】
- 款式词：sectional, modular, chaise, shaped
- 规格词：seat, seater, 尺寸数字
- 材质词：corduroy, upholstered, foam
- 颜色词：black, grey, beige
- 品牌词：boneless（如适用）

【五点描述优先埋】
- 卖点词：comfort, comfortable, soft, plush, deep, spacious
- 材质词：fabric, foam, corduroy
- 功能词：seating, lounge, support
- 场景词：space, home, living, room
- 属性词：assembly, required, easy

【长尾词组合】
- modular sectional sofa
- cloud couch living room
- deep seat sectional
- corduroy sofa couch
- l shaped sectional
- u shaped sectional
- modern sectional sofa
- comfy cloud couch
